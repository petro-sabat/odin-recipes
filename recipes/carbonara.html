Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Guanciale
  • Parmigiano reggiano
  • Egg yolks and whole eggs
  • Spaghetti
  • Pepper
  • No salt in sauce
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Batons
  2. Finely grate
  3. Sauce
  4. Cook pasta
  5. Reserve pasta cooking water
  6. Cook guanciale
  7. Toss pasta in guanciale
  8. Transfer into sauce bowl
  9. Add 1/2 cup pasta cooking water
  10. Mix vigorously
  11. Serve immediately in warm bowls.
